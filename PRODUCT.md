# Product

## Register

product

## Users

W10 maintenance and administrative staff use this dashboard during daily operations to check work order status, purchasing progress, and overtime totals for each work group.

## Product Purpose

The product gives W10 teams a fast, readable operating view of work orders, procurement, and overtime data sourced from Google Sheets. Success means users can find the correct operational page quickly and read the relevant totals without mixing employee and contractor data.

## Brand Personality

Operational, direct, familiar. The interface should feel like a working dashboard for repeated internal use.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-style landing pages, decorative motion that slows the task, and combined views that force users to scan unrelated worker types.

## Design Principles

Keep navigation explicit.
Separate operational concepts when the data is owned or reviewed separately.
Preserve the existing dashboard vocabulary so users do not need to relearn the app.
Prioritize dense, scannable tables over decorative layouts.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use readable Thai labels, clear focusable controls, sufficient color contrast, and motion only for state feedback.
